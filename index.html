Research

My research interest lies in understanding Quantum Field Theories - the language of modern physics. Specifically, I am impressed by the duality between different theories and the clarity obtained from using the powerful mathematical formalisms to acquire deep insights and derive concrete, testable results from them.

For my master’s project, I am studying the effective field theory approach to gravitation under the guidance of Dr. Suneeta Vardarajan, Associate Professor, IISER Pune. In this analysis, I have studied the formalism of Non-Relativistic General Relativity to model the waveforms of the gravitational waves emitted by various binary sources.
